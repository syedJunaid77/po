What We Do

Medicare can be overwhelming, confusing, intimidating, and at times, frustrating. How much will my prescriptions cost? Will my doctors accept my insurance? Are there other programs that can help with my healthcare costs? All of these questions can make Medicare seem like a tall mountain to climb. Whether you are already receiving Medicare or about to enroll for the first time, we are here to help you or a loved one climb the Medicare Mountain. Please don’t do it alone, we are here to help! Our goal is to assist you with finding the best plan to fit your healthcare needs.

We do not offer every plan available in your area. Any information we provide is limited to those plans we do offer in your area. Please contact Medicare.gov or 1-800-MEDICARE to get information on all of your options.
